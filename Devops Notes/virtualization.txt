				VIRTUALIZATION

process of partitioning a physical server into multiple virtual servers

Hypervisor-> a software under which the process of partioning take place

after partitioning, virtual servers act as physical servers

	Advantages->

enhancement in performance
optimum use of resources
space saving
better security
overall cost saver

	Drawbacks
heavy resouce overhead
slow boot time
increase infrastructure cost
limited application portability
higher management
inefficient scalability

These drawbacks give birth to the concept of containerization

offering ->
	lightweight isolation
	faster deployment
	better portability
	seamless integration with DevOps
	microservices achitectures
